Goofs

In the promotional picture for the series it shows a shirtless Oliver. The scars from his time on the island are visible, but there is no tattoo. During the series Oliver has a tattoo on his chest, a tattoo that plays an important role in at least one episode (1x03 "Lone Gunmen") and has been visible in every other episode. See more »

User Reviews

 
Batman and Arrow
14 November 2012 | by antobehan (Ireland) – See all my reviews

O No not another Batman rip off.After watching the pilot and sticking with the show the similarities are identical,but i cant help myself from enjoying what this show has to offer.As a big fan of Batman and having to contend with films far and few between,Arrow is a show that fills the void in between. The story and caricatures are nicely done with big budget action scenes.Each episode ends with a cliffhanger of sorts something that hero based shows lacked since the days of the Batman TV show.I was surprised to read some of the negative reviews here regarding the show.I my opinion i wouldn't let this influence your decision to watch what could be a really great show.Lets hope the writers continue to delve into the story behind his skills and the mysterious Island he returned from. 8/10
